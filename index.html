Important - Vanilla Friendly:
All features work without any mods. You can join and play on completely vanilla Minecraft. Everything is handled server-side with plugins only.

If the server prompts you to download a resource pack, it is purely for Quality of Life visual improvements: shortened or lower shields (better visibility during PvP), better grass textures (no ugly exposed dirt sides), enhanced shield models, smoother overall looks, and other small tweaks that make the game generally prettier and more enjoyable. It does not change gameplay mechanics. It only makes vanilla look nicer.
